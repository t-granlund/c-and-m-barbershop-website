Our Story

Twenty years of good haircuts, and we're just getting started.

C&M was founded in 2005 by Charles (the Barber) and Marie (the Cosmetologist) — two Northwest Arkansas lifers who'd already been cutting hair in the region for years before they ever hung the C&M sign.

For two decades we called Bella Vista home. In June 2025, we crossed the state line and settled into a new space in Pineville, MO — same chairs, same family, same warm welcome, just a little easier to find. (Look for the Taco Bell. We're right behind it.)

Today, our team of six barbers and stylists brings 80+ years of combined experience to every chair. Men, women, kids — everybody's welcome, and no appointment is required. We're here to support your vision and help you unlock your inner shine.
